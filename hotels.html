Travel

  • Arriving by Car:
    • Lots 10, 13, 14, 21, 25, and the North Garage are open for free, as long as you leave by the following morning.
    • A map can be found here or through search on Google Maps.
  • Arriving by Plane:
    • We recommend arriving at either Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) or Philadelphia International Airport (PHL).
    • Both airports are connected by train to Princeton, but EWR is a bit closer.
  • Arriving by Train:
    • Take the NJ Transit Northeast Corridor (NOT Amtrak's Northeast Regional) route to Princeton Junction.
    • Transfer to the "Dinky," which travels between Princeton Junction and Princeton Station (on campus).
    • Princeton Station is your desired final destination.
    • Exact train times and fares can be looked up on the NJ Transit site.
    • From NYC:
      • Take the subway (1/2/3/A/C/E) to NY Penn Station at 34th St and 7th Ave.
      • Take the NJ Transit (NE Corridor) to Princeton Junction (Trenton-bound).
      • Transfer to the "Dinky" upon arriving at Princeton Junction to reach Princeton Station.
    • From Newark Airport (EWR):
      • Follow signs for the AirTrain, the inter-terminal monorail, and take it to the RailLink station.
      • Upon arrival, head up the stairs to the left and cross the bridge to the train station.
      • Take the NJ Transit (NE Corridor) to Princeton Junction (Trenton-bound).
      • Transfer to the "Dinky" upon arriving at Princeton Junction to reach Princeton Station.
    • From Philadelphia (including PHL):
      • Take the train to 30th Street Station (the main station in Philly).
      • Transfer to the SEPTA Trenton Line Regional train to Trenton Transit Center.
      • At Trenton, take the NJ Transit (NE Corridor) to Princeton Junction (Newark/NYC-bound).
      • Transfer to the "Dinky" upon arriving at Princeton Junction to reach Princeton Station.
